## Purpose, Audience, and Scope Limitations

**Purpose of document:**
This document supports preliminary technical risk review for institutional decision support regarding the trustworthiness of bio/medical AI repository artifacts.

**Intended audience:**
Technical leadership, procurement reviewers, clinical informatics teams, governance officers, and regulatory affairs staff conducting due diligence on open-source bio-AI tools.

**Out-of-scope use:**
- This document is not a regulatory clearance document.
- This document must not be used as sole procurement approval evidence.
- This document does not constitute clinical validation or legal assessment.
- Scores reflect LLM-executed rubric evaluation, not independent expert certification.
